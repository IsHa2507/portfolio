About Me

I am a Computer Science Engineering student specializing in Artificial Intelligence, with a strong foundation in software development and a passion for creating impactful solutions. My expertise lies in frontend development, where I bring ideas to life using technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, alongside a growing interest in Angular. I’ve showcased my technical and creative abilities through projects like CriX, a comprehensive cricket-focused platform, and an award-winning educational platform for master's and GATE aspirants, which earned 2nd place in the 'INNOVATE X HACKATHON.'

With a keen eye for user-centered design, I possess foundational skills in UI/UX design, primarily working with Figma to create intuitive and visually appealing layouts. My experience in design complements my technical capabilities, ensuring that the solutions I develop are both functional and user-friendly. I am eager to continue enhancing my design skills as I explore new opportunities.

I am also an active leader and collaborator, balancing my technical pursuits with creative and organizational roles. As the core head of Soch Art, I’ve led initiatives that blend technology with innovation, and I’ve successfully organized events like "InnoVenture," a vibrant ideathon encouraging out-of-the-box thinking. These experiences have helped me develop leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities.

With a foundation in machine learning and Python from my internship at the Centre of Excellence - Artificial Intelligence, IGDTUW, I am skilled in integrating data-driven insights into projects. I aim to combine my technical expertise, design foundation, and leadership capabilities to contribute to innovative solutions that positively impact users and communities.
